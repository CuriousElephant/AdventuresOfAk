    • Iapetus

    Iapetus is similar in size to the other moons, apart from Titan. It has a large ridge of mountains running along it's surface, giving it the appearance of a walnut.

    If Iapetus were in fact a large walnut, you can hear what sound it would probably make if it were cracked. Please keep in mind that although a moon sized walnut would be louder when cracked, the distance the sound has to travel would mute it some.

    I have found video of a cult, decended from the one that believed the moon was made of cheese, that plans on destroying Iapetus for it's walnut rewards. It is eerie how excited they seem.
